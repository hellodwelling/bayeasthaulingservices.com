About Us

Bay East Hauling Services is Centreville, MD's most trusted junk removal service. With honesty, trust, and transparency we are your local go to for all your junk removal services and light hauling needs.

Located in Centreville, Maryland we serve QUEEN ANNES COUNTY- Stevensville, Chester, Grasonville, Queenstown, Sudlersville, TALBOT COUNTY- Cordova, Easton, St. Michaels, Tilghman, Oxford, Trappe, CAROLINE COUNTY - Henderson, Greensboro, Ridgeley, Hillsboro, Denton, Preston, Federalsburg , while also serving parts of ANNE ARUNDEL COUNTY including Annapolis, Arnold, Cape St Claire, & Edgewater areas. We specialize in residential and commercial property cleanouts, foreclosure, rental property and storage unit cleanouts, hoarder help, removal of furniture, appliances, hot tubs, mattresses, construction debris and various unwanted items, demolition of decks, sheds, swings sets. Additionally, we focus on various types of hauling with our wench equipped trailers such gravel, mulch, soil and various other items.

Please feel free to contact us for any of your hauling needs and experience small business services at it's finest.
